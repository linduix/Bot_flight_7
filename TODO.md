- **Robustness via domain randomization (food for thought)**: agents overfit to specific paths; to survive garbage/antagonistic targets, generate procedural chaos every eval epoch:
  - *Step-function teleportation*: every few dozen ticks, snap the target to a random position — forces instant recovery from huge error spikes without destabilizing.
  - *Frequency/jerk injection*: target oscillates via high-frequency sine / high-acceleration noise — simulates a target actively trying to break the control loop.
  - *Physics overmatch*: make the target faster/more agile than the drone in ~20% of episodes — forces a "best-effort / optimal corner-cutting" strategy instead of giving up when it physically can't keep up.

Makes the setpoint physically reachable each tick so max-effort = max score, and pairs naturally with physics-overmatch randomization (rewards best effort when the target can't be matched).
